# BITS Goa Course Registration and Grading Guidelines

## Course Registration Process

### Overview
The Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Goa offers a comprehensive course registration system for students. This document outlines the procedures, guidelines, and important information for course registration.

### Eligibility
- Students must be enrolled in a degree program at BITS Goa
- Minimum CGPA requirements may apply for certain courses
- Prerequisite courses must be completed before registering for advanced subjects

### Steps for Registration
1. Login to the Student Information System (SIS)
2. Select semester and academic year
3. Choose courses from the available catalog
4. Check for time conflicts and prerequisites
5. Submit registration form
6. Pay fees if applicable

### Course Load Limits
- Minimum: 12 credits per semester
- Maximum: 21 credits per semester
- Additional credits allowed with advisor approval

## Grading System

### Grade Points
BITS Goa uses a 10-point grading system:

| Grade | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| AA    | 10     | Outstanding |
| AB    | 9      | Excellent   |
| BB    | 8      | Very Good   |
| BC    | 7      | Good        |
| CC    | 6      | Average     |
| CD    | 5      | Below Average |
| DD    | 4      | Poor        |
| F     | 0      | Fail        |

### CGPA Calculation
- CGPA = (Sum of Grade Points × Credits) / Total Credits
- Semester GPA calculated similarly for that semester

### Grading Guidelines
- **AA**: Exceptional performance, deep understanding
- **AB**: Strong grasp of concepts, minor errors
- **BB**: Good understanding, some conceptual gaps
- **BC**: Adequate knowledge, needs improvement
- **CC**: Basic understanding, significant gaps
- **CD**: Minimal understanding, major deficiencies
- **DD**: Very poor performance
- **F**: Failure to meet minimum requirements

### Re-evaluation
- Students can apply for re-evaluation within 2 weeks of result announcement
- Fee: ₹500 per course
- Only marks can be re-evaluated, not grades

## Academic Policies

### Attendance Requirements
- Minimum 75% attendance required
- Medical leave can be excused with documentation
- Below 75% may result in detention or grade penalty

### Withdrawal Policy
- Courses can be withdrawn within first 4 weeks
- 'W' grade assigned, no effect on CGPA
- After deadline, 'F' grade if failed

### Repeat Courses
- Failed courses must be repeated
- Better grade replaces previous in CGPA calculation
- Maximum 2 attempts per course
